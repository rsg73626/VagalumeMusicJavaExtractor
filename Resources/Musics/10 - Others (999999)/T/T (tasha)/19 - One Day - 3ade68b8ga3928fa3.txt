ONE DAY

We huhoenun onjena pallihedo nudnun gonji
to haruga jinaganunde
ni senggagi nal te nomu manha
aphumul hado manhi gyokosonji
naege jal hejwodon gu saramul mod inun gabwa

Non otoni ijenun jinelman hani
handongan dullidon ni soshig tumhejini
nal ijodan senggage gwenhi sounhejyo

Ijenun modunge dallajyojiman sewol hurundwie
wenji dashi hanbon bogo shiphun saram
noegenun nega doeossumyon he

Gu amuri wonhago gu amuri saranghedo
nul tokathun sunun obnunde
ni senggagi nal te nomu manha
noege sangchorul jun dedgainji
noboda johun saram ajigdo mannaji mothesso

Non otoni ijenun da ijun goni
namanul saranghalgoradon
gu marul nan soguronun ungunhi
midgo shiphonabwa

Ijenun modunge dallajyojiman sewol hurun dwie
dashi bogo shiphun guron saramuro
noege nan giog doeossumyon he
