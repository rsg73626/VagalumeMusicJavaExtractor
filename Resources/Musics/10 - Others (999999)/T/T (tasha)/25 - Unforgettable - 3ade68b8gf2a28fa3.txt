UNFORGETTABLE

Yojum ote manhi himduldamyonso
onuldo nol anun chinguga ne yegil jonhadongol
guge mwoya jarhenegiro hessumyonso
niga aphulsurog nega mianhe
ne olgurul to ollyobwado gurohge sulphujin anha
nomu manhi saranghessonundedo
nomankhum irohge gasumi mudyojige doe

To hansaramdul ijoganabwa
nol algijone guressodushi
shiganuro oton aphumdo jiunungol
to hansarami chajaondamyon
nol dehadud jarhejugejiman

Nado ijhyojindanunge shirhungol
uri gute jomdo akyoyahesso
irohge heojilgoramyon jogum dol
saranghalkol ige mwoya
jinagan myotheui ilduri
modu nowaui chuogdul puningol
ne irumul yegihaltedo jonchorom uljinun anha
amuildo halsu obsonundedo
guredo irohge jogumshig na ajigedoe

To hansaramdul ijoganabwa
nol algijone guressodushi
shiganuro otonaphumdo jiunun gol
to hansarami chajaondamyon
norul dehadud jarhejugejiman

Nodo nal inundanunge duryowo
ibyorirange gurongojanha
tomillyogadud gyondyonedaga
gakumshigun gwenhan nunmuldo hurunungod
da ijurago marun hejiman
guron nol bomyon sogsanghessulkoya
ore guriwohejwoso gomawo
