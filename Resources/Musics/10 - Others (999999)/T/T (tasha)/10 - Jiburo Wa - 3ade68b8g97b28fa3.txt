JIBURO WA

Jiburo wa

Jiburo wa jibe amudo obso
yejon gathumyon jigum noege nan jonhwahaltende
nachorom mangsoriji malgo nega guriwojilte
non gunyang nege jonhwarhe jwo

Jinan dwie dashi senggaghe bomyon
we gurohge modunge ashwibgeman nukyojinunji
jogum do jarhe juji mothan majimag giog temune
nol senggaghamyon mami apha

Ajigdo nujun gon aniya
to iroda dashi bol jin molla
heojil gosul uri morun godchorom

Ajigun kuthnan ge anin gol
do soroga hengboghagi wihe
gyolgug darun gil teghejiman

Nan chaji mothesso noui gyotheso dwidora ganun gu girul
ajigun nege irun gol ibyorirago marul hagienun
ajigun naege

Ajigdo nujun gon aniya
to otohge dallajil ji malla
heojil gosul uri morun godchorom

Ajigun kuthnan ge anin gol
to iroda soro iji mothe
dashi mannal ji morujanha

Nan chaji mothesso noui gyotheso dwidora ganun gu girul
ajigun nege irun gol ibyorirago marul hagienun
ajigun naege

Dashi hanbonman to ollyo bwado
no senggaghe bwado amurohji manhun nal morodo
gutekajiman nowa naui ibyorul ibyorira senggaghaji anha
