SHIGANUN NUNMULGWA HURUGO

Uriga heojin gu nal kuthiramyo ttonadon nal
jebal niga nal jabajugil baresso
jongmal ne mamul mollanni babochorom nan dwidora
hangorum dugorum anigil baramyo ttonagasso

Shiganun nunmulgwa hurugo ulda jichyoso jamdulgo
norul ijuryogo tto darun ie giden nal
ijewa nege dorawa non uri heojin baro jon nallo
dorawa dallago amuri sori jillodo nujosso

Teronun ni senggagi na babochorom ssawotdon nal
jongmal nan norul manhi saranghennabwa
hajiman non chuog soge ne mamsog giphun bimillo
molle sumgyo nol ppun ije nonun naui sarang anya

Ije non ne mamsog bimillo jigum sarange gideo
jolde itji mothal namane chuoguroman
no anin i sarange gide nol ijogago issonnunde
we jigum nal chajawa nal sulphuge hanunde saranghe
