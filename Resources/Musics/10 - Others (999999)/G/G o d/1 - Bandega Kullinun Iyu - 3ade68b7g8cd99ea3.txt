BANDEGA KULLINUN IYU

Du saram irohge soro darujimanyo
modun ge wanjonhi jongmal bandejimanyo
hamke issul temyon wenji cham johun goryo

Jamkan ochomyon hanun jidulmada
mwonga gurohge pitaghaji deche welka
namduri hanun dero haji deche we jakku jorolka
saramduri modu chyodabonunde bulpyonhajido anha
shingyongi suil tende bodun maldun gwenchanha
anim ilbuto shisonul kullo
guromyonso soguronun ungunhi julgyo
irohge nawa darun sarami issonunji
ochom moributho dari kuthkaji darun sarami issulka
jongmariji choum bwasso gureso boge dweji
nollasso jongmal shingihesso
nawa darun ge gugol barabonun ge johasso
nodo nal barabomyonso guronji we nal baraboji oh

Saranghal god gathayo urin soro nomu darujimanyo
sarangun cham shinbiroun gojyo
saragamyonso hanbon olka malkahan guron saranginde
dagaon god gathayo kumkudon mosubun anijimanyo
sororul udge mandurojujyo
ni gyothe issumyon jakkuman hengboghadan senggagi duljyo
saranghal god gathayo

Nanun norul gamanhi bogo issumyon ihega anga
johun girul nwadugo we nappun gilman gollasoga
domuji iheharyo hedo nomu nawanun dalla
guronde we guronji norul barabogo inunge joha
modun ge nawanun domuji madnun bubuni hanado obnunde
no gathun edul nul pihagiman hamyonso saranunde
we nomanun gurohge mibgona shilhjiga anhunde
noege mwonga isanghange inunde

Banderaso do kullina nawa darunikka
guge nal do sarojabna choum bongonikka
hanado madjiga anhaso meil meil saumpunil tende
bogo shiphojyo onjena dodeche ihega andwe
pajina bwa na jogumshig noege ne gasum twinun gol bwabwa
onuse na moruge naui maumi yollyosso
norul hyangheso mangsoriji malgo durowa oso bangawo

sarangun cham shinbiroun gojyo

dagaon god gathayo kumkudon mosubun anijimanyo
