YUJOE

Nal du saram modu saranghandanyo irom andweyo
gurom nega jongmallo nappun jalmosul hangojyo
ije nukkyoyo mianheyo

We haphil gathun nal haphil dul da nal saranghandani
ige musun nalbyorag gathun iriya
nege jincha nal saranghamyon ottoghe we ne ibjangul gonranhage
manduni nanun noui mamul morunun gollo hamyon andoeni
jalmoshi anijanha nega morundamyon soguron nohiga ottohge senggaghagon

Nal du saram modu saranghandanyo irom andweyo
gurom nega jongmallo nappun jalmosul hangojyo
ije nukkyoyo mianheyo

Gure mianhe sashirun nado ara
almyonsodo morunche hago shiphonunjido molla
igijogiosso aju ne hengbogul wiheso
sangchobadul goralmyonso esso hoepihesso
hajiman gureso irohge gyolgug du saram da jinshirhan
gasumul yolgo naege saranghanda gobeghago marasso
ijen kuthnasso nega jalmothesso du saramege da

Nal du saram modu saranghandanyo irom andweyo
gurom nega jongmallo nappun jalmosul hangojyo
ije nukkyoyo mianheyo

Saranghandanun marun cham johun marijyo
du sarami dongshie hagi jonkajinyo
ijen nan ottoghajyo
ne igijogin mami gyolgug irohge narul aphuge manduljyo
