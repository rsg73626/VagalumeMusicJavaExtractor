HEOJIMBODA APHUN GURIUM

Modun ge boyo ijeya nukkyo
omlana nal saranghenunji
nomuna gakai issoso
ne nuni mollasso olmana tugbyorhanji

Ottoghe nomu nujosso
non imi obso ije kedaraso
waboni non bolsso tonasso
dashin ol su obnun gosuro michigesso
jongmal nomu bogo shiphoso
irohge hago tonaborimyon ottohge
baboya we ni mamdero gyoljonghe
ige nal saranghesoya nal wihangoya
nan norang issoya sandanmarya

Ah heojimboda do aphun gurium
ah ijeya nukkyojinun nol irhun i sulphum

Dorabwasso nol chaja hemesso
niga issulmanhan gosun modu
kog mannaya hesso halmari issosso
niga tonagoso kedarungo

Jongmal tugbyorhedon goya nomuna gwihedon sarangiya
ije doraboni dashinun mothmannal gurongoya
nomuna himduroso kuthkaji
gaji mothalgod gathdago boryoson andwesso
uriga nomu mollado nomu mollasso
heojiji marassoya hesso jolde i mal haryo hedongonde
dudio nol gyou chajanunde non ije obso
ottoghe ilbunirado do uri hamke issoya hesso
jongmal ilbunirado do uri hamke issoyahesso

guronikka niga nal wiheso hedanun iri
olmana mari andoenun irini darunsarami
ni jaril no deshin hullyunghi mekkwo
nal hengboghage halgoragu
narul bandushi noboda hwolshin
johun saram mannage handagu
nuga nobogu senggaghe dalle
nega deche wonhanunge mwonde
narul hengboghage hanunge mwonde
gugol we pesso guge baro nonde
