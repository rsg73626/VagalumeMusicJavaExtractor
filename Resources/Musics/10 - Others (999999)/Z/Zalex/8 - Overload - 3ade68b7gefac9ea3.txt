OVERLOAD

You can discuss so many themes, 
Propose a solution, 
Rise in associates' esteem. 
You concentrate at global schemes, 
Free substitution... 
But you don't see under your feet! 

Overload... 
Your overload of mind 
Makes you blind. 

Invention, fiction, fantasy... 
Why can't you see under your feet? 
Your global view, your global ride, 
Your local blindness of your mind. 

Overload... 
Your overload of mind 
Makes you blind.
