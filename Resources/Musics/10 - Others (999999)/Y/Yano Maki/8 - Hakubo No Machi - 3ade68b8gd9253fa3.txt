HAKUBO NO MACHI

hi ga shizumu sakamichi, SUMOGGU ni kemuru machi
daisuki na kono basho e  tooi mukashi yoku kita

ano koro no watashi e iki wo kirashinagara
  mou ichido kakete yuku  imasugu

tasogarete yuku sora, me ni utsuru keshiki ga
  umarekawatta mitai ni kagayaiteru
kono machi ni umarete
  kono kaze ni tsutsumare  watashi wa ikite kita no

wasuretai kanashimi ga  koko e to hakonda no?
natsukashii nioi ga  wake mo naku setsunai no

kawatte yuku watashi wo douka mimamottete
  tomadoi mo yowasa mo terashite

ORENJI ni nurareta  yane ya kawa ya michi ga
  katarikakete kureru you ni kagayaiteru
kono chiisana sekai ga yoru e to mukau koro
  itami mo kiete iku yo

tasogarete yuku sora, me ni utsuru keshiki ga
  umarekawatta mitai ni kagayaiteru
kono machi ni umarete
  kono kaze ni tsutsumare  watashi wa ikite iku yo
