SARANGI JEOMANCHIGANE

Sarangi jeomanchigane 
na hollo namgyeo nokoseo 
sewora meomchwobeoryeora 
naenimi gaji motage 

Sarangi tteonagandane 
ibami da jinagamyeon 
uriui majimak siganeun 
butjabeul su neun eopgetji 

Ije ibyeorui sigani dagaone 
sarangi tteonagane 
naneun jugeodo neoreul itjineun motalkkeoya 
achimimyeon tteonallima
