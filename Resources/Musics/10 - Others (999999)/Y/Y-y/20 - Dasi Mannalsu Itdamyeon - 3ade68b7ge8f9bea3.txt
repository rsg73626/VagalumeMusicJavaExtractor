DASI MANNALSU ITDAMYEON

Eonjenga neon naege malhaetji 
geureoke dasi mannandamyeon 
geunyang utjago amumaldo pillyoeobseo 

Hajiman ne moseup gieongnajireul anha 
ara bolsuisseulkka jasini eobseo 
antakkawo nunmurina 

Gin sigan jina ne ape seolttaeeneun 
nan seulpeunpyojeongeul jitgo sipjianha 
uri tto dasi mannalsuman itdamyeon 
geureoke doelsuman itdamyeon 
gaseum gadeukhi neoui pume angieo 
jami deulgo sipeo
