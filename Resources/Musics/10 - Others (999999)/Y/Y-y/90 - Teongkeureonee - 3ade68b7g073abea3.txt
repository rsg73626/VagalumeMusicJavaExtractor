TEONGKEUREONEE

eeshweeoon keot eopshee modeen sarameen cheok 
meeryeoneopshee ponaenok'o 
nameun sarangmank'eum kot'ongdeuldo 
ooseum twee-e soongyeo noasseo 
keukkat han saram ssaweon ddeonan keon na saneun tongan 
kakkeum keolleeneun hannat yeolbyeongeelppoon 
hamkke haettdeon naldeuldo kot hyoongt'eo hana eopshee 
amooneun kabyeo-oon sangcheo jagoogeejeeman 
jeecheen nae haroo-e kkeut'e keo-ooree peechoon 
keep'ee pae-een sarancheoe nan noonmoolman teongkeureonee 
neomoo keureeweoseo mollae hanbeon 
keu eereum pooreun twee tashee kareeneun eolgool

eetgee heemdeureoseo ap'eun chooeokdeureul 
nongdamcheoreom neureo noattjee 
keukkat han saram ddaweon ddeonan keon na saneun tongan 
kakkeum keolleeneun hannat yeolbyeongeel ppoon 
hamkke haettdeon naldeuldo kot hyoongt'eo hana eopshee 
amooneun kabyeo-oon sangcheo jagoogeel ppoon

keep'ee pae'een sangcheoe nan noonmoolman teongkeureonee 
neomoo keureeweoseo mollae hanbeon keu eereum pooreun twee 
tashee kareeneun eolgool 
neol ponael soo eopneun nal almyeonseo 
nal eetneunda ddeonan neo 
eeje jogeumssheek heosoolhaejeen 
kamyeon heulleo naeryeo hyoonghan nal pogettjee 
keuddaejjeumen jogeumeerado amooreojyeo eesseoseo 
neol pomyeon soomjee ank'eel
