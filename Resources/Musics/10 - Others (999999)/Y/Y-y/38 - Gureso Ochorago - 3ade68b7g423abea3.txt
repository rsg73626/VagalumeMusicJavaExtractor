GURESO OCHORAGO

Namjadabji mothage 
unun mosub shilhdago 
udnun naman gioghalsu idorog 
nunmul chamurago 

Monjo tonagedoeso 
jongmal mianhajiman 
shiganhullo himihejiltekaji 
jogumman gyondirago 

Gureso mol nabogo ochorago 
irohge ganungol gunyang durago 
amugodo nan byonhage obnunde 
ajig saranghanunde 
noman irohge 
honja byonheboryonunde 

Selsuobnun giogdul 
niga namgin modun hunjogdul 
sajingadug udgoinun ni olgul 
ijenda ijurago 

Gureso mwol nabogo ochorago 
irohge ganungol gunyang durago 
amugodo nan byonhange obnunde 
ajig saranghanunde 
noman irohge honja byonheboryonunde 

Charari modun ne sarangi shigdorog 
ilburo naege chagabji gureni 
soro aphujianhge 
jogumirado yegamhalsu ige 
junbihalsu ige 

No jongmal ironsaram anijanha 
irohge heojil uri aniya 
namjadabji mothe mianhajiman 
oso gu balgil dollyo 
ulgo inun nal irukhyo juryom 
no obshin muodo 
halsu obge doeborin na
