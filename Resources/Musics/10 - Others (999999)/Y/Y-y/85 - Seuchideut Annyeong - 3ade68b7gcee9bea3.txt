SEUCHIDEUT ANNYEONG

Geurae baro nayeyo geudaega muchaegimhage beorigo gan saram 
wae geuri nollanayo hanbeonjjeumeun majuchil sudo itjyo 
geu eosaekhan pyojeonghajima yeope geunyeoga utgo itjanha 
geudaepareul kkok butjapgo inneun geunyeo maneul saenggakhae 
amuil eopdeusi seuchyeoga jwoyo 
hanbeonman deo mujeonghamyeon doeneunde gwaenhi insa marayo 
naege mianhandeut geu nunbitto sirheo 
seuchideut annyeong haeyo 

Jogeumssik dagaoneun geudae. yeope geunyeoreul baraboayo 
pyeonanhandeut geudael mitneundeutaeyo 
naega geureohaetdeusi 
amuil eopdeusi seuchyeoga jwoyo 
hanbeonman deo mujeonghamyeon doeneunde gwaenhi insa marayo 
naege mianhandeut geu nunbitto sirheo 
seuchideut annyeong haejwo 

Geunyeoegen naege hangeotcheoreom doraseoji marayo 
geuge eolmamankeum himdeunirinji amudo moreulgeoyeyo 
geudaeraneun saram itneungeon nado ajik
