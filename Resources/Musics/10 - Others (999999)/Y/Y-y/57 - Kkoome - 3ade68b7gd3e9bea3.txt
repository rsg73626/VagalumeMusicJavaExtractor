KKOOME

naneun mosheejeo 
ponjeokdo eopgo eereumdo moreuneun 
jeenankkoom seuchyeogan sarameeyeo 
ee pam komgomee saenggak'ae ponee 
eodeeseonga pondeut'an paro keu moseub

eoneuhae mannattdeon sarameeyeo 
eoneu kaeul nanoodeon sarangeeyeo 
nan nooneulddeumyeon kkooomeseo kkaelkkabwa 
nan noon mootddeugo keudaereul ponae 
moolgeop'oomcheoreom kkaejyeobeoreen nae kkoomeeyeo 
oneulbame keudaeyeo wayo
