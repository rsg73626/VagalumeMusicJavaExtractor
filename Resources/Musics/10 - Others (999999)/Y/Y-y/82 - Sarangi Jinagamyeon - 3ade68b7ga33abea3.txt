SARANGI JINAGAMYEON

Geu saram nareul boado naneun geu sarameul mollayo 
dugeungeorineun maeumeun apado 
ijen geudaereul mollayo 
geudae nareul arado naneun gieogeul motamnida 
mogi meeowa nunmuri heulleodo sarangi jinagamyeon 
geureoke bogosipdeon geu eolguri geujeo seuchyeo jinamyeon 
geudaeui heotalhan moseupsoge 
na ijen huhoeeobseuni
