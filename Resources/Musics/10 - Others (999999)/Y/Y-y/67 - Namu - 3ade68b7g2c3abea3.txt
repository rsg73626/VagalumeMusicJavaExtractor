NAMU

Ajigeun manhi himdeureoyo siganeun amureochi anheundeut 
chagapge jugeumboda ssaneulhage jichin gaseumsoge meomulgo 
geudaereul miwohal su eomneun hyeonsireul naengjeonghage malhaejujyo 
sesangi dasi nal anajuneun nal ije dasi eobseugeotman gata 

Naneun geudaeui maeume jaraneun aju yeorin namuyeosseoyo 
idaero geunyang sideulji anke geudaeui songilman gidaryeo watdeongeojyo 

I mankeum ppalli jaranaseo geudaega swil su inneun geuneulgwa sonagi 
geudael jeoksil su eopdorok pume gadeuk
