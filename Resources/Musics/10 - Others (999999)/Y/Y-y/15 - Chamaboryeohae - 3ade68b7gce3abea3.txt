CHAMABORYEOHAE

Da ijeobeoryeo jiwobeoryeo 
nal moreuneun sarami doeeojwo 
neul mugeowotdeon urisarang ijeneun kkeutnae 
cheoeumbuteo saranghalsu eomneun neoreul mannaseo 
sungmyeongcheoreom gipi ppajyeodeureo 
gyeolguk hanyeojareul ulligo marasseo 

Every night I cry Oh baby you hurt me and her too 
ttodareun sarangui iyureul aesseo seolmyeonghajima 
uriui inyeoni namaseo ttodasi mannage doendamyeon 
geuttaeneun namanui yuilhan namjaro dorawajwo 
na himdeureodo chamaboryeohae sigani neol deryeogagetjiman 
nan geureoke sarajigetji 
nae mameul dolliryeo hajima neomu neujeosseo 
geunyeol jeongnihandan geojitmareul mideojulsueobseo gamdanghalsu eobseo 

Neol neomuna saranghaesseo neodo aljanha
