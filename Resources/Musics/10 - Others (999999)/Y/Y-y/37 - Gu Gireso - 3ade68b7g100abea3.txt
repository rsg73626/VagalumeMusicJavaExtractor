GU GIRESO

Gu hurodo aju oredongan 
gu girul jinalte en 
hengboghedon uri jinan iri 
jakku to olla aphahejyo 

Sarangdo ibyoldo mollassodon orin nanun 
dalkhomhan sogsagim jagun yagsogkajido 
yongwonhal goshira midojyo 

Gu gireso ochomyon gudel bol su issulka 
nado molle sumjugyo gudel gidarineyo 
jal arayo ije donun bol su obdangol 
gureyo ibyorhessunikka 

Dashinun gu gire gajianha gajianha um 
myot bonul doesegyo sulphun naui gorumul 
doedollimyo dajimhessojyo 

Gu gireso soro usumjidge hedon du saram 
nomu shwibge uri ibyorhage doel jul mollajyo 
hunukkidon narul anajul su obnayo 
gureyo ibyorhessunikka
