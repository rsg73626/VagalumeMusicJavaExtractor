sothurun ine sarange 
chag irhun sulphume 
sorowo wechinda

morojidon dwid mosubdo 
da midulsu obso 
nan guderoya na irohge

aju mongose hona gathun i hanurare 
suchidud memdolmyo himdulge wechinda
