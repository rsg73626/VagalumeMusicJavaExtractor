SARANGUL JIUDA

To yogikaji wanabwa 
igsughan gorumi narul iguni 
pyongsengul hemegedoel subgwanigeji 

Nunaphe niga tonajiranha 
gilgo gin yegirul nuro nohgonhe 
jidoghan guriume haruga chwihe 
honja namun nimosub gogjongdwe 

Sumanhun medubi ongkyoso 
heonaji mothal gioge sandedo 
norul nohajun onul harumanun 
ijhyojigirul bare 

Seroun sarang norul cheugil 
bujoghan narangol algedwessuni 
ijeya dannyomaphe nunmuri hullo 
donun norul jabulsu obnungol 

Sumanhun medubi ongkyoso 
heonaji mothal gioge sandedo 
norul nohajun onul harumanun 
ijyojigirul 

Isarang jikhyoneji mothe 
aphahal nal wihe shiganul namgyojwo 
gasumsogeso nol jiul su ige 
ijen yonsubhe bolke
