GOODBYE MY LOVE

Mundeuk malhago sipeotjyo 
uri dasi sijakhal suneun eomneunji 
jamsi ullida kkeunheojin jeonhwaedo 
naneun hoksina geudaengeol neukkyeoyo 
ijeuryeogo chingureul mannatjyo 
amu soyong eopdaneun geol nan arayo 
geu eonjenga geudae apahalttae hayake bamsaedeon 
geu chueokdo ijen naui mogsi anigetjyo 

Saranghaeyo tteonaji marayo

nan eojjeomyeon johayo 
naega meonjeo bonaeya halkkayo 
saeroun yeonini gidarinayo 
naboda deo haengbokhage haejul geot gatdamyeon
