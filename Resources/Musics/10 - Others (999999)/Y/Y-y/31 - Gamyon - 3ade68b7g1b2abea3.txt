GAMYON

Gu mogsori gu mosub noinde 
onurun niga anya 
olguri nomu nadsoro 
yogijogi todonun ni somun 
hullinun ni usumi ijen jongmal shirho 

Hanbonun shilsugo dubonun subgwaniya 
sebonun dorikhilsu obso 
jinshimul gajanghan gamyon sun nimosubul 
amudo midji anundan gol 
we noman moruni 

Dashinun huhoehal irul mandullyo hajima 
irojinunma sesangi 
noui mamdero doenungon aniya 

I baboya jebal ijen momchwo 
nado sanggwanobdago ijoborimyon doendago 
gurohjiman gugon anijanha 
gurom naui sarangdo gamyoni doejanha 

Hanbonun shilsugo dubonun subgwaniya 
sebonun dorikhilsu obso 
jinshimul gajanghan gamyon sun ni mosubul 
amudo midjianundan gol we noman moruni 

Dashi huhoehal irul mandullyo hajima 
irojinunma sesangi noui 
mamdero doenungon aniya 

Namduri noege gurorago hanungon 
niga ottohge doedun sanggwani obgie 
dalkhomhedon sarangun sun aphumman 
junun gonikka (nemalduro) 

Nuguna mannago gurohge heojigo 
nagathun saram do senggimyon (guttegaso) 
sarangdo saramdo irhulkoya 
ije gumanhe ni olgulsoge 
gamyonul bosojwo 

Hanbonun shilsugo dubonun subgwaniya 
sebonun dorikhilsu obso 
dorawa jigumdo nujungon anilkoya 
nujodo sanggwani objanha ije dorawa 

Dashinun huhoehal irul mandullyo hajima 
irojinunma sesangi 
noui mamdero doenungon aniya
