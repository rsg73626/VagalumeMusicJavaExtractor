CHAGEUN YEONMOT

kipeun san osolgil yeop chagomahan yeonmoten 
chigeumeun teoreoun mulman koigo amugeotdo saljianjiman 
meon yetnal i teonmoten yeppeun pungeo 
tumari salgo isseotdago cheonhae chichiyo 
kipeun san chageun teonmot aneu mareun 
yeoro ssaweo han marineun mulwie tteooreugo 
yeorin sari sseogeo teureoga 
muldo ttara sseogeo teureoga yeonmotsogeseon 
amugeotdo sal su eopge tweeotjyeo 

kipeun san osolgil yeop chagomahan yeonmoten 
chigeumeun teoreoun mulman koigo amugeotdo salji anjyo 
pureudeon namushipi nanip tuip tteoreojyeo 
yeonmotwie chageun pae ttuiudoga kipeun soge karaaneumyeon 
chip ireun kkotsaseumi san sogeul hemaedaga 
teonmoseul chajawa mureul mashigo salmyeoshi chamdeulge twejyo 
haeneun seosane chigo cheonyeok saneun koyohande 
sanheoriro mudangbyeolle hana hwiik chinagan hue 
cheomeun mulman goin chae haneopneun 
seweol sogeul mareopshi momjiseuro 
hemaedo suimaneun kyejeoreul matjyo 
kipeun san oseulgil yeop chageumahan yeonmoten 
chigeumeun teoreoun mulman koigo amugeotdo salgi anjyeo
