okubyou na kimi wa hoho wo nurasu kedo oboeteite 

tooku hanareteite mo kokoro wa hitotsu de unazugiteiru kimi ni 
nemurenai yoru naraba utatte ageru kara mitasarenai omoi wo "uchi akete"
