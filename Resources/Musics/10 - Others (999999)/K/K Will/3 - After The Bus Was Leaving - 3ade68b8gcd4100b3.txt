AFTER THE BUS WAS LEAVING

(neo ttaemune haengbokhajiga anha...)

Heeojyeodallan mareul haengbokhaji anhdan mareul
Kkeutnae ni ibeseo deutgo marasseo...
Neomu changpihagedo walkak nunmuri heulleoseo
Meonjeo ireoseoseo bakkeuro nagasseo...

Jibeuro ganeun jeongryujang... Ajikdo tteollineun simjang...
Geuman geu jarie jujeo anjasseo...
Beolsseo beoseureul myeot daessik 
bonaego saranghan neol bonaego
Ibyeoriraneungeol silgamhaesseo...

Nal tteonajima... Nareul ullijima...
Heeojijago malhajima...
Beoseun tteonago neodo tteonago... 
Dwineujge soneul heundeureodo
Ijen annyeong naui sarang annyeong
Uri jeongmal ibyeorin gabwa
Beoseun tteonasseo imi tteonasseo
Ni maeumdo da tteonagasseo... Goodbye

Rap)
Beoseun tteonago nahollo nama
Deonggeureoni nunmureun geureongi
Mideojiji anhneun mitgosipji anheun
Ibyeoreul eojjeoni nae gaseum tdulheojigo
Geu jarie geudaero
Niga ol ttaekkaji gidaryeo
You know nae kkumman gateun saranghan neol
Idaero bonael su eopsdangeol

Gyeolguk bonaebeorin munja... Uri heeojiji malja...
Bonaeji maraya hal munjayeosseo...
Neoreul bulpyeonhage mandeul geoya... 
Budamseureoul geoya...
Neujeun huhoeman nan hago isseo...

Nal tteonajima... Nareul ullijima...
Heeojijago malhajima...
Beoseun tteonago neodo tteonago... 
Dwineujge soneul heundeureodo
Ijen annyeong naui sarang annyeong
Uri jeongmal ibyeorin gabwa
Beoseun tteonasseo imi tteonasseo
Ni maeumdo da tteonagasseo... Goodbye

Nunmuri heureugo chagaun bismuldo heureugo...
Neoui moseupman... Jakku saenggakna... 
Jeongmal michidorok apeunde..... .

Neon tteonajiman... Nareul tteonajiman...
Uri birok heeojijiman...
Cham johassjanha... Haengbokhaessjanha... 
Geuttaen jeongmal saranghaessjanha...
Ijen annyeong... Naui sarang annyeong...
Neoreul bonaejwoya hana bwa...
Doraondaneun gidaen an hallae...
Ije dasin sarang an hallae..... . Goodbye
