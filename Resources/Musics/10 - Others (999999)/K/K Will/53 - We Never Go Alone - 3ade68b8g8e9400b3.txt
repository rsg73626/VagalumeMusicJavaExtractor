WE NEVER GO ALONE

Cheo-eum neol mannat-deon geunal useumyeon insahaet-jiman
Sashil jamshi meomulda seuchirinyeonil-keora
Midko shipeo-nneunji molla

Keuddaen nu-gudo midji mothaet-deon nal
Oraen shi-gan yeopeseo jikyeojun neol
Eoneusae jo-geumsshik seumyeodeu-reo nae ma-eumeul yeol-ko
Wehrob-ji anheul su it-dorok hamkke haejun neo

We never go alone idaero yeongwonhi
Ni-ga yeope isseoseo himdeun shi-ganeurikyeonael su isseo
Chigeumcheoreom jabeun soneul noh-ji mal-go we never go alone

Keuttae-neun neodo mollasseul-keol
Uri hamkke hal-keoran geol
Kakkeum nae-ga miwoseo urin yeo-gikkajira-go
Nun-mul heullyeosseul-ji molla

Nae gaseum so-ge maejhyeo i-nneun neo
Neoye nun-mul so-ge beonjyeoi-nneun na
Sumanheun yaegideu-reun seoroye mideumeul mandeul-ko
Himdeuneot-deon shi-gan gyeoteseo hamkke haejun neo

We never go alone idaero yeongwonhi
Ni-ga yeope isseoseo himdeun shi-ganeurikyeonael su isseo
Chigeumcheoreom jabeun soneul noh-ji mal-go we never go alone

Honjaseo noheul su eop-seosseo nal mideojun
Narikkeu-reojun ni-ga isseo ganeung-haesseo
Jichyeo ul-koshipdeon naldo neul gyeote isseoseo
Useul-su isseosseo

We never go alone idaero yeongwonhi
Ni-ga yeope isseoseo eodu-un i gi-reul hechyeo-gal su isseo
Chigeumcheoreom jabeun soneul noh-ji mal-go we never go alone
