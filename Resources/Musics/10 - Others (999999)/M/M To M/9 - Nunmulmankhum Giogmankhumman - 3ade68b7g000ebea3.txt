NUNMULMANKHUM GIOGMANKHUMMAN

Onjena baraman bogo isso hangorum dwie 
babochorom amumaldo haji mothanche 

Himgyobge junbihanun insajocha gonnejimothe 
babochorom udgoinun nega shilhne 

Negyothe dan hansaram jongmal nega doelsu obnunji 
jogumman nal bara bolsu obnunji 

Ijuryo hedo daguchyodo bwajiman 
dashi chaorunun guriume nunmulman 
nomani salge hanungol negen jonburanungol 
ne barem mankhum gajil sun obsodo nol saranghanikka 

Gakkumshig nal bomyo usojudon haruga nege 
sesang gajang hengboghedon shiganiyosso 

Na anin gu saramgyothe inun nol bomyon 
haruedo sumanbonshig dannyomhessoji 

Ne gyothe dan hansaram jogmal nega doelsun obnunji 
niane nal bada julsun obnunji 

Ijuryo hedo daguchyodo bwajiman 
dashi chaorunun guriume nunmulman 
nomani salge hanungol negen jonburanungol 
ne barem mankhum gajil sun obsodo 

Hanaman no horaghedwo nege ol sun obgejiman 
nunmul mankhumman giog mankhumman saranghalke 

Nol mironego darun sarang hebwado 
boriji mothe ajigdo nan nunmulman 
doraol saram gathaso jakku dora bogedoe 
ne yogshimchorom gajilsun objiman nol saranghanikka 
nol giog hanikka nol sarang hanikka
