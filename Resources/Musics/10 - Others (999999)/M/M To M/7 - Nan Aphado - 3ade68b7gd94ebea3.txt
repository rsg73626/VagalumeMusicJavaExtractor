NAN APHADO

Modu da ne jalmoshingol ijen nohajulke 
dwido bojima urisarang igose borigo 
onjena non narul tonalkabwa jobashimul nesso 
nega nomudo bujoghedon sarangirangol algie 

Norul jiujin anhulkoya 
norul ganjighamyo salkoya 
gu giogdullo nan pyongsengul aphado gurohge namun narul saragalke 
ni gioge nunmul hulligo gu nunmure na jiwojyodo 
norul aphuge han gu borul badumyo harul saragalkoya 
gu boldullo
