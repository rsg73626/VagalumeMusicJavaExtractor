PA BO

Non yojum manhi bapunga bwa narur mannarsu obsur mankhum
gakum jonhwar gorodo non bapu damyo kunhoji
guron nega nan gogjongi doe hogshina aphun denun obnun gonji
manhi himidur temyon nor sarang hanun nar gakumun sengaghe julle

Nan guromyon doe nan gugo punya do isang muodo nan nege wonchi anha
ihe harsu isso gidarirsu isso norur nomu sarang hanika
nor bogo shipho jom himdurodo hyonshire jichyo ganun norur nukir temyon
nor handongan mo bonun naboda do nega gogjongi doe

Ne chingu gakum jonhwar goro nasson yojawa inun norur
nege marhejujiman nan gogjonghaji anhaji
teron gurorsu issur koya jonbutho argo jinen chingur koya
nega jamdurgi jone nar sarang handanun gu hanmadi man hejulle

Iron narur bogo we saramdurun da babora hanunji molla
nan midgo shipho ne sarang manun gu nuga muorago nege marur hedo
norur bara bonun naui sarang manun hangsang gathun gose inunde
nan bogo shipho nor yejon chorom hajiman jonhwajocha harsu obsur mankhum
no gurohge yojum do himdunga bwa nega gogjongi doe
