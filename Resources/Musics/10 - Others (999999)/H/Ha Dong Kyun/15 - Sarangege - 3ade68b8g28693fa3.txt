SARANGEGE

Saranga gareuchyeojullae naega mweol jalmothaenneunji
wae jamshi meomuldaga seuchyeoganeunji
nae gaseumeul jjijeononeunji
saranga kkok daedaphaejullae yunanhi nal gwerophineunji
manheun saramjunge wae nareul goreunji
nae nunmureul bogo shipeungeonji
love eonjekkaji nae nuneul garil geonji
eolmana nae mogeul umkyeojwilgeonji
marae love dubeon dashi nae gyeote ojima
neoreul bangyeojul mamdo neoreul anajul himdo eopseuni
saranga kkok daedaphaejullae yeongweonhi nal gadweodulgeonji
haetssal hana eomneun neoraneun gamogi
nae summajeo ppaetkko shipeungeonji
love eonjekkaji gogaereul nureul geonji
eolmana nunmureul bi-euseul geonji
marae love heorageopshi nae pume sumjima
neoreul bodadeumeul himdo eopsseuni
saranga naega chamji mothago
tto dashi neoreul chajado
moreuneuncheok gago isseojweo forever
