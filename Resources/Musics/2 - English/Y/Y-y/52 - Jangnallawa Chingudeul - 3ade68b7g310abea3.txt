JANGNALLAWA CHINGUDEUL

Neol algo nan hu eolmana apahaenneunji 
neul ne gyeoteul maemdolmyeo honjaman sangcheo badatji 

Nal anajugireul eolmana baraewanneunji 
i bam kkumeul kkuneungeol nae sarangi doen neoui moseup 

You and I will be the one 

Nan mitgo inneungeol eonjengan naesarang irwojilkkeorago 
nae jinsim kkok arajulkkeorago 

Ije nan eotteokhani neo eomneun sarmeun saenggakhal su eomneunde 
nae modeun geon imi ni geosingeol 

Neo wonhaji anhado nan imi jeonghae beoringeol 
nae modeun sarang neoege bachigiro 
jebal nae mameul badajwo 

You and I will be the one 

and don? let me go away 

Nal oemyeonhajima baraneungeon eobseo 
geujeo ne gyeote meomulgo sipeo 

Ije nan eotteokhani neo eomneun sarmeun
