IRUM

Kog ijulge hana namji anhge 
no choum bon gunal gioghaji 
tonadallamyo hullin ni nunmure 
nan gwenchanhunchog jashin inunchog 
usumyo yagsoghenunde 

Amuri sewori hurugo hullodo jiwojiji anhun irum 
ijen nunmullo ne gasume namun norul shiso bollyogo 
onuldo bamsewo ulgo to urodo 
do kojyoman ganun irum 
nenune ne dubore jagun nesone 
jakkuman bonjyoman ganun irum 

Da ijumyon guten hengboghalka 
nol choum bon gunal giogmankhum 
gujo insado badun ne irumman 
moruge doemyon nadsolgedoemyon ne modunge ijojilka

guthorog sorichyo burugo bullodo 
na gajilsu obnun irum 
ijen charari narul jiwo 
norul modu ijul su ige 
irohge himdulge wonhago wonhedo 
na dahulsu obnun irum 
jo hanul wihesonun 
tonan nol manna dashinun sarangul halsu ige
