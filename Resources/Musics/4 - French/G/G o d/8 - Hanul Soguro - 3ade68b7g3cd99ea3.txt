HANUL SOGURO

Nunmurul dakkayo uljinun marayo
bol su obda hedo sarajiji anhjyo
uri mam soge yogi irohge yongwonhi sara sumshwigo ijanhayo
amado dashinun iron gippumdurun nukkil su obgejyo nomu hengboghejyo
ne insengeso choegoui sunganul gudewa hamkehejyo

Gomawossoyo ne gyothul jikhyojwoso nugudo jul su obnun sarangul
guthorog oren shigan hengboghessoyo iji mothal goyeyo nungamnun nalkaji
ne mame yeppuge segyodun che salgeyo
