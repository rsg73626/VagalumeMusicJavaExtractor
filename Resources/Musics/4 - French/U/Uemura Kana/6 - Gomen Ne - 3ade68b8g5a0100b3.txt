GOMEN NE

Gomen ne itsumo shinpai kakete sonna 
tsumori wa nai ndakedo
Watashi ga suki katte suru to nazeka 
anata wo okorasete shimau

Gomen ne itsumo hanpatsu shite nakanaka 
sunao ni kikenai kedo
Honto wa wakatte iru kara omotte kurete iru koto

Hito no kimochi tte muzukashii na betsu ni 
komarasetai wake ja nai no ni
Doushite darou umaku tsutawaranai
Kitto mata meiwaku kakechau narubeku ki wo tsukete iku kara
Onegai zutto soba ni ite ne

Gomen ne konna watashi dakedo risouzukashitari shinai de ne
Anata ga inai sekai nante kangaeta dake de namida ga dechau

Gomen ne unzari shiteru kana dakedo hitotsu dake iwasete ne
Keshite anata wo uragiranai koredake wa shinjite ite

Hito no kimochi tte muzukashii na zutto nakayoku 
shitetai dake na no ni
Doushite darou itsumo kenka bakari
Iya ni naru toki mo aru darou kedo soko wa hitotsu 
nagai me de mite
Issho ni toshi totte ikou ne

Gomen ne soshite itsumo arigatou
