IT'S NOT YOU

Neol cheom bwasseul ttaen kkumeul
kkun geot gatatgo du beon bwasseul
ttae unmyeongiran geol arasseo
geureoke neoege ppajyeoseo nae
jeonbureul bachyeosseo jungneun
geo ppaegon mwodeun da haesseo geuttaen

neol gajyeosseul ttae nan sesangeul
da gajyeotgo neo hana isseumyeon
neoman isseumyeon nan chungbunhaesseosseo
geunde geuraetdeon niga naege
jeonbudeon niga naege simjanggwa gatdeon niga
nal tteonandani naega eotteol geot gata

* niga anin geot gata kkok ttansaram gata
maeil nae pumeseo angyeo utdeon
niga nal tteonal ri eobtjanha
geu yejeonui neol chajeul su
eobtjanha niga geojitmal gata

kkumil geoya

eotteon iyugeon hamkke hagiro haetjanha
geu manheun yaksogeun manheun naldeureun da eotteokharago
naege bichideon niga naege sarmideon niga
naegeneun duldo eopdeon niga
nal tteonandani naega eotteol geot gata

* Repeat

'geumanhaja' mameul ttaerineun geu mare
on sesangi meomchwobeorin geot gata

naneun motal geot gata andoel geotman gata
neoreul ape dugo ulkeokdaeneun naega
injeonghal su eobtjanha
nal tteonaneun neol chameul su eobtjanha
nareul dugo gaji ma jebal dugo gaji ma
