WAKE

Yeonrakhal sarami eopsne
jigeum i sigane nan
ani ohaeneun haji mara
jigeum mak kkaen geonikka
geunyang deo puk jamdeulmyeon johasseul tende
jeokdanghan geon himdeureo

gipge janeun geon deo himdeureo
eonjekkaji na ireohge sarado doelneunji
momeun jebeop pyeonhan geot gateunde
maeumi an pyeonhae

i jeongdomyeon nal al ttaedo dwaessjanha nal
bakkuneun geon uimi eopsjanha
nareul injeonghae i jeongdomyeon nappeuji anha

ijen nal saranghagiro hae
nugunga sarangeul mutgi jeone
dareun saramboda meonjeo
nal aranae meonjeo
nae mameul deureobwa
