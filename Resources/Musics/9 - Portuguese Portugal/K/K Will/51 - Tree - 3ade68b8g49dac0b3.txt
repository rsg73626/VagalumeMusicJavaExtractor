Tteonalsuga eobseoyo i-jarie seoseo
ppurinaerin gulgeun namu cheoreom
geudae tteonan goseseo hangeoreumman umjigyeobwado
bari apawayo

gyejeori amuri nareul jinado
naegaji jocha kkeogji mothajyo

eonjenga geudae doraoneun eonjenga
dasi nareul chatneun eonjenga
bandeusi ol geu nal. nal chajeul su itge
nae dupareul deo beolligo daejireul gulgge didgo
geudael hyanghae nan jaralkkeoeyo

nunmuri nalttaemada naeaneuro gadeuk samkyeobwayo
naege mureul judeut uri chueogdo naegen
hanyeoreumui haessalgwa gatjyo
nal jaranage hajyo

geudae dwireul ttara geodji mothaedo
geudae swil geuneureul junbihajyo

eonjenga geudae doraoneun eonjenga
dasi nareul chatneun eonjenga
bandeusi ol geu nal. nal chajeul su itge
nae dupareul deo beolligo daejireul gulgge didgo
geudael hyanghae nan jaralkkeoeyo

mogeul jwaeneun taneun yeoreumdo sareuraeneun gyeoulbaramdo
nan gyeondilkkeoeyo i jarie seoseo
mideoyo geudae doraogil mideoyo
naege dasi ol geol mideoyo
nugudo i mideum ppaeasseul sun eobtjyo

taetjuri mareugijeone nae simjangi ttwineun nareun
mideulkkeyo doraogireul oo
gidaryeoyo i-jarie seoseo
